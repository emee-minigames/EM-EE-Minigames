ABOUT:

So EM-EE Minigames is a game inspired by EPIC Minigames. This game is just made by 1 young child and this website is made by me as well ;D [HOPE YOU LIKE IT!]

This is the first game which is officially released — the other games are just trash and not really worked on.

EM-EE Minigames has 2 teams: EM- represents Fox and EE- represents Crown.

People can host events by making challenges of who will reach the end and get first!

The first minigame of EM-EE Minigames was SpinningTopTower and the second was MountainClimb.

There are different zones in which you can go by completing very difficult minigames. For example, you can enter the Champion Realm by completing the TryhardObby of Newbie Realm.

The currency of the game is called 'EM-EE Bit Coins', which can be obtained by completing different types of minigames.

NOTE: Not all minigames give you coins — some are just made for fun!

With the 'EM-EE Bit Coins', you can purchase an EM-EE Card which allows you to buy special stuff. You can also purchase tools with Bit Coins.

In the future, I will add FREE UGCs in exchange for money. Therefore, please support the game! Thank you and have a great day!
